About Me

Nafis Piyal

Major: Software Engineering

I am a senior at North Dakota State University, expected to graduate in May 2024. Currently, I am working as an undergraduate research assistant, where I specialize in writing code for custom 3D printers and various embedded systems. My academic journey has been defined by a passion for hardware, particularly GPU and CPU architectures.

I'm fascinated by how these components work and how they can be optimized to enhance performance, particularly in accelerating AI workloads. Exploring the intersection of hardware and artificial intelligence is where I find myself most engrossed.

Outside of my academic and research pursuits, I'm an avid programmer. I enjoy taking on personal coding projects, whether it's experimenting with new languages or building something innovative from scratch. It's through these projects that I continue to hone my skills and expand my knowledge in the field.

Looking ahead, my dream career revolves around CPU/GPU architecture, hardware engineering, or software engineering. I'm particularly drawn to companies such as NVIDIA, AMD, and Apple due to their prominent role in shaping the future of hardware. The opportunity to contribute to groundbreaking advancements in technology is something I aspire to be a part of.

In summary, I'm a driven and passionate individual with a love for all things tech-related. Whether it's through my research, coding projects, or aspirations for the future, I'm constantly striving to make a meaningful impact in the world of technology.
